Sesame Chicken

Sesame chicken is a popular Chinese-American dish featuring bite-sized pieces of tender chicken coated in a savory and slightly sweet sauce. The chicken is typically first coated in a mixture of soy sauce and cornstarch before being fried to a crispy golden brown. The finishing touch includes a sprinkle of sesame seeds, adding a nutty flavor and a delightful crunch to this delectable dish.

Ingredients:

    chicken breast
    soy sauce
    cornstarch
    sesame seeds

Steps:

  • coat chicken
  • fry chicken
  • make sauce
  • serve
