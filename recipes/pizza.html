Pizza

Pizza is a beloved Italian dish that consists of a thin or thick crust, topped with tomato sauce, melted mozzarella cheese, and various toppings. It serves as a versatile canvas for creativity, allowing for a wide range of flavor combinations. Common toppings include pepperoni, vegetables, and various meats, providing a satisfying and flavorful experience with each slice.

Ingredients:

    pizza dough
    tomato sauce
    mozzarella cheese
    pepperoni slices (optional)

Steps:

  • prepare dough
  • add sauce
  • spread sauce on dough
  • sprinkle mozzarella
