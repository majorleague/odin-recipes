Glazed Donut

A glazed donut is a classic and indulgent sweet treat enjoyed around the world. Made from a simple dough of flour, sugar, and yeast, the donut is fried to golden perfection and then coated with a glossy glaze made from confectioners' sugar. The result is a light and fluffy interior encased in a sweet, shiny exterior. Glazed donuts are a timeless comfort food, perfect for satisfying a sweet tooth craving any time of the day.

Ingredients:

    flour
    sugar
    yeast
    confectioner's sugar

Steps:

  • prepare dough
  • shape and fry
  • make glaze
  • put glaze on donuts
